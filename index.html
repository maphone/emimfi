    Our operations are guided by six key social goals


    01

    EMI offers responsible financial services, enabling clients to sustainably grow their household and business assets and achieve their family goals.

    02

    EMI offers first-rate financial education services, helping clients learn to manage their money wisely in meeting their household budget needs and growing their businesses.

    03

    EMI is an equal opportunity employer that values diversity and invests in the continuous education of its employees.

    04

    EMI follows microfinance industry best practice and works closely with partners and peers to ensure sustainable growth and offer quality financial inclusion in Lao PDR.

    05

    EMI seeks long-term sustainability through financial viability while creating client value.

    06

    EMI cares about the environment and takes measurable steps to reduce its carbon footprint.
